MET Bhujbal City

MET League of Colleges, Bhujbal Knowledge City, Nashik (also known as MET Bhujbal Knowledge City), is a private institute. It was established in 2006 in Nashik, Maharashtra. The courses offered by the MET Bhujbal Knowledge City are approved by AICTE and PCI. The institute facilitates UG, PG, Doctorate and After 10th programmes in Full Time mode. A total of 30 courses are offered across various specialisations.
